Temple Run 2

Created2025-09-12|Updated2025-09-14|Running Games
|Post Views:

Developer: Imangi Studios

Released: January 2013

Platform: PC, Mobile, Tablets

Genre: Endless Runner, Casual

Features: Enhanced graphics, new obstacles, power-ups, character upgrades

About Temple Run 2

Temple Run 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the groundbreaking endless runner game that took the world by storm. Developed by Imangi Studios and released in 2013, this action-packed sequel builds upon the success of the original Temple Run with improved graphics, new environments, challenging obstacles, and exciting gameplay enhancements.

In Temple Run 2, you once again take on the role of a daring treasure hunter who has stolen a priceless golden idol from an ancient temple. Your mission is to escape the crumbling temple complex while being relentlessly pursued by ferocious demon monkeys. As you race through perilous cliffs, dense forests, and dark mines, you’ll need to use your quick reflexes to jump, slide, and dodge obstacles to stay alive.

With its intuitive controls, vibrant visuals, and addictive gameplay, Temple Run 2 offers an exhilarating experience that keeps players coming back for more. Whether you’re a fan of the original or new to the series, this game delivers endless hours of entertainment.

Gameplay

Temple Run 2 retains the core gameplay mechanics that made the original so popular while introducing exciting new features and challenges.

Main Objectives

  • Escape the temple complex while being chased by demon monkeys
  • Collect coins to purchase power-ups and character upgrades
  • Navigate diverse environments including cliffs, forests, and mines
  • Avoid obstacles such as gaps, barriers, fire traps, and crumbling platforms
  • Unlock achievements and compete for high scores

Game Flow

Your character automatically moves forward through the temple complex at an ever-increasing speed. You must use your reflexes to make split-second decisions to avoid obstacles and stay ahead of the pursuing monkeys. Each run is procedurally generated, ensuring a unique experience every time you play.

As you progress, you’ll encounter new challenges and obstacles that test your skills to the limit. The game continues until your character crashes into an obstacle, falls into water, or is caught by the demon monkeys.

Highlight Features

Enhanced Graphics and Environments

Temple Run 2 features stunning high-definition graphics that bring the temple complex to life. Explore diverse environments with detailed textures and dynamic lighting effects that create an immersive gaming experience.

New Obstacles and Challenges

Face a variety of new obstacles that weren’t in the original game, including zip lines, mine carts, and rotating fire traps. These new challenges require quick reflexes and strategic thinking to overcome.

Power-Ups and Upgrades

Collect coins to purchase and upgrade powerful abilities that help you survive longer:

  • Shield: Protects you from collisions with obstacles
  • Coin Magnet: Attracts coins from a distance
  • Boost: Propels you forward at high speed
  • Invisibility: Makes you temporarily immune to obstacles

Character Customization

Unlock and play as different characters, each with their own unique abilities and characteristics. Customize your character with various outfits and accessories to personalize your gaming experience.

Power Meter

Fill the power meter by collecting coins during your run. Once full, it triggers a temporary boost that grants increased speed and invincibility, helping you evade dangers and achieve higher scores.

Achievements and Leaderboards

Complete a wide range of achievements to earn rewards and prove your skills. Compete with friends and players worldwide on the global leaderboards to see who can achieve the highest score.

Game Controls

Temple Run 2 features simple and intuitive controls that make it easy to pick up and play:

Control Action
A/D or Left/Right Arrow Keys Move left or right to change lanes or turn at intersections
W or Up Arrow Key Jump over gaps and obstacles
S or Down Arrow Key Slide under low-hanging barriers

On mobile devices, you can use swipe gestures with the same functionality.

Tips and Tricks

Master the Basics

Practice the basic controls until they become second nature. Being able to jump, slide, and change lanes quickly will help you navigate the temple complex more effectively.

Collect Coins Strategically

Prioritize collecting coins that are easy to reach without risking a collision. Use the Coin Magnet power-up to maximize your coin collection.

Anticipate Obstacles

Look ahead and anticipate upcoming obstacles. Jumping early at the edge of a cliff or preparing to slide under a barrier can make all the difference.

Time Jumps Carefully

For obstacles that require precise timing, such as double jumps or avoiding multiple obstacles in quick succession, practice your timing to ensure you clear them successfully.

Upgrade Your Abilities

Use the coins you collect to upgrade your power-ups and abilities. Focus on upgrading abilities that align with your playstyle for the best results.

Complete Stage Objectives

Work towards completing the stage objectives to progress through the game and earn additional rewards.

A: Yes, Temple Run 2 is completely free to play on our website without any downloads or payments required.

Q: Does Temple Run 2 have an ending?
A: No, Temple Run 2 is designed as an endless runner game, meaning there is no official end. The game continues until your character crashes, falls, or is caught by the demon monkeys.

Q: Can I play Temple Run 2 on PC?
A: Yes, you can enjoy Temple Run 2 directly in your browser on our website, making it accessible and free to play on any PC.

Q: Is Temple Run 2 online or offline?
A: Playing Temple Run 2 on our website requires an active internet connection.

Q: What are the monsters in Temple Run 2?
A: The primary adversaries in Temple Run 2 are the Evil Demon Monkeys, sinister creatures that relentlessly chase the player to reclaim the stolen golden idol.

Q: What is the power meter in Temple Run 2?
A: The power meter fills as you collect coins during a run. Once full, it triggers a temporary boost, granting increased speed and invincibility to help you evade dangers.

Q: How do I upgrade powers in Temple Run 2?
A: Visit the “Abilities” menu to enhance specific powers like Shield Duration, Coin Magnet, or Boost Distance by selecting and upgrading your preferred ability.

Why Play Temple Run 2?

Temple Run 2 takes everything that made the original game great and amplifies it with stunning graphics, new gameplay mechanics, and exciting challenges. Whether you’re looking to beat your high score, compete with friends, or simply enjoy an action-packed gaming experience, Temple Run 2 delivers on all fronts.

The game’s simple controls make it accessible to players of all ages and skill levels, while its increasing difficulty and variety of challenges ensure that even experienced gamers will find it engaging. With procedurally generated levels, no two runs are ever the same, providing endless replay value.

So why wait? Strap on your running shoes, grab the golden idol, and embark on an unforgettable adventure in Temple Run 2! Can you outrun the demon monkeys and escape the temple’s clutches?
