About Level Devil - NOT A Troll Game

Level Devil - NOT A Troll Game is a 2D platformer that looks innocent at first glance but quickly reveals its true nature: a gauntlet of devilish traps and mind-bending obstacles. You play as a brave adventurer navigating through a series of increasingly difficult stages, each packed with hazards designed to test your wit and dexterity. With 16 unique doors to unlock—culminating in the ultimate challenge of the Demon King’s area—this game is a rollercoaster of emotions, from rage-inducing fails to the euphoria of victory.

Gameplay

In Level Devil, you’ll experience challenging platforming action with the following gameplay elements:

  • Deceptively Simple Controls: Use arrow keys for movement and jumping
  • Twisted Mechanics: Some levels feature abnormal gravity or reverse movement
  • Deadly Traps: Navigate through sinkholes, sharp spikes, teleporting walls, and mysterious dots
  • Progressive Difficulty: 16 doors with 5 stages each, ramping up in challenge
  • Persistence Required: Exiting mid-game means restarting from the beginning of the current door’s stages

The game combines classic platformer mechanics with mind-bending puzzles and unexpected traps, creating a unique and challenging experience.

Highlight Features

Deceptively Simple Gameplay

Controlling your character is straightforward, relying primarily on arrow keys for movement and jumping. However, mastering these controls is anything but easy. Some levels introduce abnormal gravity or reverse movement, turning familiar mechanics upside down. You’ll need sharp reflexes and quick thinking to navigate these twists.

A Variety of Deadly Traps

Level Devil throws a wide array of obstacles your way, each more cunning than the last:

  • Sinkholes: These pits can appear suddenly or shift unexpectedly, ready to swallow your character
  • Sharp Spikes: Stationary or mobile, these hazards demand precise timing to avoid
  • Teleporting Walls: Walls that materialize out of nowhere can push you into danger
  • Mysterious Dots: Some act as platforms, while others spell instant doom
  • Reverse Controls: Levels that flip your controls add a layer of confusion to the chaos

These traps, combined with other surprises, ensure no two stages feel the same, keeping you on your toes throughout the journey.

Progressive Difficulty

With 16 doors, each containing five stages, Level Devil ramps up the challenge as you progress. Early levels ease you into the mechanics, but by the time you reach the Demon King’s area, you’ll face puzzles and traps that demand near-perfect execution.

Skip Level Option

For those moments when a level feels insurmountable, Level Devil offers a “Skip Level” feature. After a few failed attempts, you can watch a short ad to bypass the stage. While this accessibility option is handy, the real satisfaction comes from conquering each challenge through persistence and skill.

  1. Stay Alert: Expect the unexpected - traps can appear suddenly or behave unpredictably
  2. Observe Patterns: Watch how traps work before making your move
  3. Practice Precision: Many traps require pixel-perfect timing and positioning
  4. Learn from Mistakes: Each failure teaches you more about the level’s tricks
  5. Be Patient: Rushing often leads to mistakes - take your time to analyze each situation
  6. Use Skip Wisely: The skip feature is there to help, but don’t overuse it if you want the full challenge
  7. Master the Basics: Even simple movement can be tricky in later levels
